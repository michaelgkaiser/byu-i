About Me

Michael G. Kaiser

Nickname: MH

Hello friends! Here are a few things about me. I was born and raised in the very south of Austria, but I currently live in Salt Lake City, UT. I served a mission in the England Manchester Mission, and after returning home I met my wife, Mindy, after moving to Innsbruck, Austria to go to school. We moved to Utah close to where Mindy grew up and started our family. We have four children, the oldest of which just started at BYU-I on campus in Rexburg, ID, and we are now racing to see who will finish first with a Bachelor's degree.

About a year ago I started to once again pursue my dream of finishing school. I loved the PathwayConnect program and I have been so impressed with the instructors and classes I take through the Pathway program. I work in the printing industry, and part-time at a hotel. I do enjoy working hard, but I truly love the outdoors and doing something and anything active.

By going back to school I am working towards a career change. Something inside of me constantly pushes me to learn something new. Anything to do with computers seemed to be so complicated to me, but the more I learn about programming the more it interests me.

Although I have been a member of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints all my life, my faith and testimony constantly evolve, and like with programming - the more I learn about our Heavenly Father and His plan for His children, the more I want to know Him and about Him. I am grateful for every opportunity that is given to me to learn more and deepen my understanding.

Good luck to all of you!
